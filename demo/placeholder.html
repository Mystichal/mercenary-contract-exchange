The Problem

Trust is scarce.
The frontier doesn't have time for it.

⚠️
No enforcement
Mercenaries take payment and vanish. Tribes issue missions with no guarantee of execution.
📉
Underpriced risk
No market exists to price the difficulty of missions — territory, threat level, logistics.
🔒
Locked capital
No secondary market means no liquidity. Accepted contracts can't be reassigned or traded.

What we built

Core Infrastructure

  • ◆ Pre-funded escrow — reward locked on-chain before mission starts
  • ◆ Transferable obligations — execution rights can be bought, sold, reassigned
  • ◆ World-state verification — killmails, status events, cargo deposits as proof
  • ◆ Automatic settlement — no arbitrators, no trust required
  • ◆ Contract registry — on-chain index for UI discovery without an indexer
